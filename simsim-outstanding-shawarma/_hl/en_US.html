Simsim Now Hiring

At Simsim, we are creating something special: an amazing place to eat and work while learning key lessons that will carry you throughout your career. We provide exceptional training and a clear career path for career success and advancement.

What makes Simsim the right fit for you?

Free food (yes, really FREE)
Paid time off
Holiday closures
Full time and part time opportunities
Opportunities for advancement
Competitive compensation and bonus opportunities.
What we're looking for:
Someone with a friendly, enthusiastic attitude
Someone that loves to help and serve others (both customers and team members)
Someone ready to learn how to cook (a lot)

We prepare real food by hand every day in our restaurants. No cans, nothing pre-made; everything made from scratch.

We welcome experienced restaurant professionals as well as those looking to learn the business for the first time. We provide the training you need to feel confident working at any station. We take pride in our outstanding customer service by creating a friendly atmosphere that our guests expect and enjoy.

At Simsim, you'll be part of a team that is working to cultivate nourished communities where wholesome food is enjoyed every day. If that sounds like something you would like to be a part of, apply today!
