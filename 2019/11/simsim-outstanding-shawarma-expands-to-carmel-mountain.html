Simsim Outstanding Shawarma expands to Carmel Mountain

November 25, 2019

A new Mediterranean and halal spot has debuted in Carmel Mountain. Called Simsim Outstanding Shawarma, the addition is at 11640 Carmel Mountain Road, Suite 124.

On the menu, look for wraps, kebabs, bowls, salads and falafel made from scratch daily. This is the restaurant brand's second location; the first is in Clairemont. 

With a 4.5-star rating out of 33 reviews on Yelp so far, the newcomer has received solid feedback from patrons.

Mura Z., who was among the first Yelpers to review the new spot on Nov. 16, wrote, "The new location is is amazing and clean, the staff is super friendly and most importantly, the food is always outstanding. We ordered chicken shawarma with pomegranate, and it tasted beyond authentic."

And Ashley O. wrote, "Came here tonight for dinner and can't wait to come back. So delicious and very sweet staff."

Swing on by to take a peek for yourself: Simsim Outstanding Shawarma is open from 11 a.m.–10 p.m. on Monday-Saturday and 11 a.m.–9 p.m. on Sunday.
